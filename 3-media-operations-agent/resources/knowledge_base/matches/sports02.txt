GOLDEN EAGLES STUN PLATINUM KINGS IN OT THRILLER, 133-130
March 22, 2024 - Thunder Dome

World Arena Series - In a breathtaking finish at the Thunder Dome, the Coastal Golden Eagles overcame the Empire Platinum Kings in an overtime classic that had fans on their feet throughout the final minutes. With the scores locked at 125-125 in regulation, Eagles' star guard Jason Rivera (#7) in the team's signature yellow jerseys, orchestrated a masterful overtime performance against the white-clad Kings. The tournament quarterfinal matchup lived up to the "Let the Strongest Win!" banner that adorned the packed arena, with both teams trading leads until the Eagles pulled away in the final minute.
